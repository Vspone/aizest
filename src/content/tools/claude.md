Anthropic Claude is a next-generation AI assistant developed by Anthropic, designed to excel at writing, analysis, coding, and complex reasoning tasks. With a massive 200,000-token context window, Claude can process and analyze entire books, lengthy research papers, or extensive codebases in a single session. The model places a strong emphasis on helpfulness and safety, making it a reliable partner for professional content creation and deep analytical work.

Claude's writing capabilities span blog posts, articles, essays, creative fiction, technical documentation, and business communications. It adapts naturally to different tones and styles, and can refine content through back-and-forth dialogue. The assistant also supports file uploads, allowing users to drop in PDFs, Word documents, images, and spreadsheets for summarization, analysis, or rewriting.

For developers and analysts, Claude offers robust code generation, debugging, and data interpretation features. Its ability to maintain context over extremely long conversations makes it ideal for multi-step projects and iterative workflows. Available on web, iOS, and via API, Claude is a versatile tool that competes at the highest tier of AI writing assistants.
