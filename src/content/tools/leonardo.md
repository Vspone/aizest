Leonardo AI is a creative-focused image generation platform that has carved out a strong niche in game asset creation, character design, and concept art. Built on a foundation of fine-tuned Stable Diffusion models, Leonardo offers an accessible yet powerful interface that appeals to both beginners and experienced AI artists. The platform's standout feature is its focus on generating consistent game-ready assets — characters, environments, weapons, and items — with a style that fits seamlessly into game development pipelines.

The AI Canvas tool provides inpainting and outpainting capabilities, allowing users to extend or modify existing images with precision. The real-time generation feature is particularly impressive, showing the image being painted progressively as you type or adjust settings, giving instant feedback that speeds up the creative iteration process. Leonardo also hosts a community feed where users share their creations and the model presets used, making it easy to discover new styles and techniques.

Leonardo offers a generous free tier with 150 daily tokens, making it one of the most accessible platforms for serious experimentation. Paid plans start at $10/month for the Apprentice tier, which adds faster speeds, private generation, and API access. While it may not match the pure photorealism of DALL-E 3 or the artistic polish of Midjourney, Leonardo's specialized toolset for game assets and its user-friendly approach make it a favorite among indie developers and digital artists.
