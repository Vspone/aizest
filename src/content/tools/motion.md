Motion is an AI-powered productivity tool that solves a very real problem: the time spent manually managing your calendar. Instead of manually blocking time for tasks and shuffling them when meetings appear, Motion automatically schedules your work around your existing commitments. Tell it what tasks you need to complete and by when, and Motion finds the optimal slots on your calendar, adjusting dynamically as new meetings appear.

The tool combines project management with calendar management, so you can track project deadlines, dependencies, and team workload alongside your personal schedule. For busy professionals juggling multiple projects and meetings, Motion's auto-scheduling is timesaving — the $19/mo individual plan pays for itself in reduced scheduling overhead within weeks.
