ChatGPT has become the default AI tool for millions of users worldwide. Its versatility is unmatched — you can write a blog post, debug code, analyze data, brainstorm ideas, and generate images all in one conversation.

The platform's recent updates have focused on making it more useful for professionals. Custom GPTs let you create specialized assistants for your specific needs without any coding.

For most users, the free tier is surprisingly capable. The $20/mo Plus subscription unlocks the full GPT-4o experience and removes most limitations — well worth it if you use it daily.
