Speechify is the most popular text-to-speech application globally, used by millions of students, professionals, and accessibility users. Its killer feature is OCR-based reading: point your phone camera at a page of text — a book, a receipt, a whiteboard — and Speechify reads it aloud in natural-sounding voices within seconds.

The Chrome extension makes it effortless to listen to articles, emails, and web pages instead of reading them. Combine this with cross-platform sync (your reading progress follows you between devices), and Speechify becomes a genuine productivity multiplier. The Premium voices are remarkably natural — far beyond the robotic TTS of a few years ago. For anyone who wants to consume more content while commuting, exercising, or doing chores, Speechify is the go-to tool.
