Perplexity replaces traditional search engines for many users. Instead of getting a list of links, you get a comprehensive answer with footnotes linking to the sources — making it ideal for research and fact-finding.

The follow-up question capability is what sets it apart. You can dive deeper into any topic through natural conversation, and Perplexity maintains context across your session. It's like having a research assistant who can search the entire web in real-time.

Pro mode ($20/mo) unlocks the most capable AI models and unlimited searches. For students, researchers, or professionals who need to gather information quickly, Perplexity is invaluable.
