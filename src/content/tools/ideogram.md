Ideogram is the go-to AI image generator for anyone who needs crisp, readable text inside their visuals — marketers, social media creators, logo designers, and branding professionals. While most AI image models struggle with typography, rendering letters as garbled nonsense, Ideogram achieves roughly 90% text accuracy out of the box. This makes it the clear leader for use cases like poster design, social media graphics, presentation slides, event flyers, and logo concepts where legible text is non-negotiable.

The platform runs on Ideogram 4.0 models, offering three generation tiers to match different needs: Turbo for rapid ideation, Balanced for everyday use, and Quality for polished final outputs. Beyond pure generation, Ideogram provides practical editing tools including Magic Fill inpainting for targeted edits and background replacement, plus a character reference feature that maintains consistent characters across multiple images. For developers and businesses, the API enables integration at a per-image pricing model, and the team plan supports collaborative workflows with shared credit pools and admin management.

Pricing runs from a genuinely useful Free tier (10 slow credits per week) to Plus at $15/month annually (1,000 priority credits), Pro at $42/month annually (3,500 priority credits), and Team at $20/user/month. The main caveats are that priority credits expire monthly with no rollover, maximum output resolution is capped at 1024×1024, and even on paid plans, "slow credits" can mean significant wait times during peak usage. For anyone who needs AI-generated images with text that actually looks right, Ideogram is the strongest choice available today.
