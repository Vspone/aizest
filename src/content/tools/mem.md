Mem is an AI-powered note-taking platform that reimagines how knowledge workers capture, organize, and retrieve information. Unlike traditional note apps that require manual folder structures and tagging, Mem uses artificial intelligence to automatically organize your notes as you write. It surfaces connections between related ideas, suggests relevant content from your history, and keeps your knowledge base organized without any manual effort.

The core experience revolves around writing quickly and letting Mem handle the rest. As you add notes, Mem's AI analyzes the content and links it to existing notes, categorizes it into relevant collections, and makes everything instantly searchable. The smart search goes beyond keyword matching to understand the meaning behind your queries, pulling up results from across your entire note history even when you don't remember exact terms. The AI writing assistant can also help draft, summarize, or rephrase content directly within the editor.

Mem is particularly well-suited for researchers, writers, product managers, and anyone who needs to manage a growing body of interconnected information. The ability to start with a free plan and upgrade to Pro for advanced AI features makes it accessible for individual use, while the Business tier adds team workspaces and shared knowledge bases for organizational deployment. It represents a genuine shift from manual knowledge management to AI-assisted thinking.
