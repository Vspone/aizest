Writer is an enterprise-focused AI writing platform designed for organizations that need to generate high-volume, on-brand content while maintaining strict governance and compliance standards. Unlike general-purpose AI writing tools, Writer allows companies to train the AI on their brand voice, style guides, and proprietary knowledge, ensuring every piece of content — from blog posts and emails to ad copy and social media updates — aligns with organizational standards.

The platform's governance capabilities are its strongest differentiator. Writer includes built-in guardrails that prevent the AI from generating content that violates company policies, industry regulations, or legal requirements. Approval workflows allow managers to review and approve content before publication, while compliance teams can set rules that the AI automatically enforces. The analytics dashboard provides visibility into content performance, helping teams refine their strategy over time.

For marketing teams at mid-sized to large organizations, Writer offers a level of control that general AI tools simply cannot match. The knowledge base integration means the AI can pull from company documentation, product specs, and approved messaging to generate accurate, up-to-date content. While the free tier and Team plan at $18 per user per month are accessible, Writer is most valuable at scale, where brand consistency, compliance, and workflow governance become critical business requirements.
