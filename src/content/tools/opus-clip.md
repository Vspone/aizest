Opus Clip solves a very specific and very real problem: turning long videos into short-form clips that work on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. It analyzes your full-length content — whether that's a podcast, interview, webinar, or YouTube video — and automatically identifies the most engaging moments. It then generates multiple short clips with animated captions, b-roll overlays, and attention-grabbing hooks, ready to publish.

The AI highlight detection is genuinely impressive. Opus Clip looks for moments of high energy, topic shifts, laughter, key insights, and other signals that suggest audience engagement. It then ranks these moments and generates clips of varying lengths. The auto-captioning feature is a standout — it creates animated, speaker-labeled captions that follow the words in real time, which is essential for the short-form video format where caption quality can make or break a clip's performance.

The main trade-off is control. Opus Clip's AI does a good job, but it doesn't always pick the right highlights, especially for nuanced or context-dependent content. The free tier is very limited — one video per week with watermarked exports — so content creators publishing regularly will need the Pro plan. For podcasters, streamers, and YouTubers looking to repurpose content efficiently, Opus Clip is a genuine time-saver.
