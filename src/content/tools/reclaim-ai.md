Reclaim.ai is the AI scheduling assistant that builds the perfect work week — automatically. By connecting to your Google Calendar or Outlook, Reclaim analyzes your schedule and intelligently blocks focus time, optimizes meeting placement, and safeguards your personal commitments.

The core idea is simple: instead of you manually carving out time for deep work, Reclaim does it for you. It learns your habits, preferences, and work patterns, then dynamically adjusts your calendar as new meetings and tasks come in. If a meeting gets rescheduled, Reclaim reclaims that time for focused work instead of leaving it empty or letting it get eaten by another meeting.

For teams, Reclaim is a scheduler that respects everyone's time. It finds optimal meeting slots considering all participants' schedules, work hours, and time zones. The work-life balance dashboard tracks how much time you're spending in meetings versus focused work, and even monitors after-hours activity to help prevent burnout. With integrations into Asana, Todoist, ClickUp, and Slack, your tasks and communications are automatically reflected on your calendar — making Reclaim the intelligent layer between your tools and your time.
