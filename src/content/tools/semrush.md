Semrush has long been the gold standard for SEO and marketing analytics, and its recent AI additions make it even more powerful. The Content Marketing Platform includes an AI writing assistant that drafts articles based on your target keywords and search intent, complete with suggested headlines, meta descriptions, and internal linking opportunities.

The AI-powered keyword research goes beyond simple volume data. Semrush analyzes search intent, identifies content gaps where competitors are ranking and you're not, and suggests topic clusters that build topical authority. The site audit tool crawls your site like a search engine and uses AI to prioritize technical fixes by their potential impact on rankings.

For serious SEO professionals and marketing agencies, Semrush is worth the investment despite its price tag. The data depth, competitive intelligence, and now the AI content features make it a complete marketing command center. Free users should check out the limited tools available, but the real value starts at the Pro tier.
