Synthesia makes video production accessible to anyone. Write a script, choose an avatar, and get a professional-looking video in minutes — no cameras, studios, or acting skills required.

The avatar quality has improved significantly. The latest generation of avatars has more natural facial expressions, better gestures, and improved lip-sync accuracy. Custom avatars let you clone a real person's appearance for consistent branding.

For businesses creating training videos, product demos, or marketing content, Synthesia can reduce production time from weeks to hours. The 120+ language support means one video can be localized for global audiences without reshooting.
