AIVA (Artificial Intelligence Virtual Artist) is an AI music composition tool designed to help creators generate original soundtracks and compositions without needing formal music training. Trained on thousands of classical and contemporary scores, AIVA can compose in styles ranging from orchestral and cinematic to jazz, rock, and pop. It is widely used by video game developers, filmmakers, podcasters, and content creators who need custom background music on demand.

The platform allows users to start from scratch or use reference tracks to guide the AI toward a desired mood or style. Once AIVA generates a composition, users can edit it note by note in the built-in editor, adjusting melody, harmony, and instrumentation. Export options include MIDI for further refinement in a DAW, as well as high-quality audio files. This flexibility makes AIVA both a rapid prototyping tool for music and a starting point for deeper creative work.

For commercial creators, the royalty-free licensing on paid plans is a major advantage — there are no ongoing royalties or copyright conflicts when using AIVA-generated music in published projects. While the free tier is quite restrictive (only three compositions per month), the Pro plan at $19 per month offers substantial value for anyone producing video, games, or podcasts who needs unlimited, custom-composed background music.
