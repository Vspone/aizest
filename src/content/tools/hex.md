Hex is a modern, collaborative data science platform that combines the flexibility of notebooks with the power of AI to help teams explore data, build analyses, and share insights more effectively. Unlike traditional data tools that force trade-offs between exploration and communication, Hex provides a unified environment where data scientists, analysts, and business stakeholders can work together in real-time — with AI assistance that speeds up every step of the analytical workflow.

The platform's core is its collaborative notebook, which supports both SQL and Python cells alongside rich text, visualizations, and interactive components. Hex's AI copilot can generate code from natural language descriptions, explain existing queries, suggest optimizations, and even help debug errors. Once an analysis is complete, users can publish it as an interactive data app or dashboard with just a few clicks — complete with input controls, parameterized queries, and permission controls. Scheduled runs and automated alerts keep teams informed without manual effort.

Hex is ideal for data teams that value collaboration and reproducibility. Its version-controlled notebooks eliminate the chaos of sharing screenshots and CSV files, while the ability to turn analyses into interactive apps bridges the gap between data exploration and decision-making. For organizations looking to build a data-driven culture where insights are accessible to both technical and non-technical stakeholders, Hex provides the infrastructure and AI assistance to make that vision a reality.
