Apollo.io is a sales intelligence powerhouse that gives sales teams access to 275 million B2B contacts with verified email addresses and phone numbers. The platform combines a massive contact database with AI-powered lead scoring, intent signals, and multi-channel outreach automation — all in one tool that replaces separate data providers (ZoomInfo), engagement tools (Outreach), and email verification services.

What makes Apollo particularly compelling is the free tier, which provides genuinely useful access to search and limited contact credits. For startups and small sales teams, Apollo's free plan is often enough to validate the platform before scaling up. The $49/month Basic plan unlocks meaningful outreach capabilities that would cost hundreds per month with separate tools.
