CapCut is a powerful, free AI-powered video editing platform developed by ByteDance, the company behind TikTok. It has quickly become one of the most popular video editing tools for social media content creators, thanks to its intuitive interface, extensive template library, and impressively capable AI features. CapCut works across web browsers, desktop applications, and mobile devices, making it easy to edit videos anywhere with seamless project synchronization.

The AI features in CapCut are where it truly shines. The auto-caption tool generates accurate, stylized subtitles with just one click — a massive time-saver for content creators targeting social media platforms where captioned videos perform better. The text-to-speech engine offers a variety of natural-sounding voices across multiple languages, and the AI video enhancement tools can upscale resolution, stabilize shaky footage, and improve low-light quality. The template library is enormous, with thousands of ready-made templates tailored to trending TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts formats.

While the free version is remarkably generous, it does add a watermark to exports. The Pro plan at $7.99/month removes the watermark, unlocks premium effects and templates, adds advanced AI features like auto-cut and motion tracking, and enables 4K export. For social media content creators, marketers, and anyone producing short-form video content, CapCut offers the best balance of power, ease of use, and affordability in the AI video editing space.
