QuillBot is the world's most popular AI paraphrasing tool, used by millions of students, academics, and professional writers. Unlike generative AI tools that create content from scratch, QuillBot excels at rewording your existing text while preserving meaning — and unlike basic thesaurus-style tools, it restructures sentences naturally rather than just swapping synonyms.

The multiple paraphrase modes let you control the output: Standard for balanced rewording, Fluency for fixing awkward phrasing, Formal for academic tone, Creative for more dramatic restructuring, and more. Combined with the grammar checker, summarizer, and citation generator, QuillBot is a complete writing toolkit. At $9.95/month, it's one of the best-value writing aids available.
