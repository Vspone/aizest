Framer is a cutting-edge AI website design and prototyping tool that lets anyone create professional, responsive websites without writing a single line of code. Originally known as a high-fidelity prototyping tool for designers, Framer has evolved into a full-fledged website builder that leverages AI to accelerate the design process — from generating initial layouts based on natural language prompts to refining visual details with intelligent suggestions.

The platform combines the precision of traditional design tools with the speed of AI automation. Users can start from a blank canvas, choose from hundreds of templates, or use Framer's AI to generate a complete site structure based on a simple description. Every element is fully customizable, with support for custom fonts, animations, scroll effects, and responsive breakpoints. The built-in CMS and SEO features make it a complete solution for blogs, portfolios, landing pages, and marketing sites alike.

For designers and non-designers alike, Framer removes technical barriers while maintaining professional output quality. Its one-click publishing and integrated hosting mean you can go from concept to live website in hours, not days. With powerful prototyping capabilities still at its core, Framer is equally suited for building production sites and creating interactive design mockups for client presentations.
