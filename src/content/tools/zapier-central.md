Zapier Central represents the next evolution of no-code automation. Beyond traditional Zapier's if-this-then-that workflows, Central introduces AI-powered bots that you can train with natural language. Instead of configuring every trigger and action manually, you describe what you want — "when a new lead comes in via email, create a CRM entry and send a Slack notification" — and Central builds the automation.

The platform connects over 6,000 apps, making it the most extensive automation ecosystem available. From email and calendar tools to CRMs, project management platforms, and AI services like ChatGPT, Central sits at the center of your tool stack. The Tables feature adds a lightweight database that can store data across automations, enabling more complex workflows like lead scoring pipelines and inventory management.

For businesses already using Zapier, Central is a natural upgrade. The AI capabilities reduce the time spent building and maintaining automations, and the Central Brain concept means different automations can share context and data. It's especially useful for sales operations, marketing workflows, and customer support routing where data needs to flow across multiple systems reliably.
