Clipdrop, developed by Stability AI (the creators of Stable Diffusion), is a comprehensive AI image editing toolkit wrapped in a clean interface. Its background removal rivals Adobe's technology and often works better on complex subjects like hair and fur. The relighting tool is a standout feature — adjust the lighting direction, intensity, and shadows of an existing image as if you had reshot it in a studio.

The clean-up tool removes unwanted objects, people, or text from photos with simple brush strokes. While the text-to-image generation uses Stable Diffusion under the hood, Clipdrop's value proposition is really about editing existing images rather than generating new ones from scratch. At $9/month for Pro, it's one of the most affordable professional-grade AI image editing tools available.
