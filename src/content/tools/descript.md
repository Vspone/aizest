Descript revolutionized video editing with a simple but powerful idea: make video editable like a document, earning it a stellar 4.6 rating from users and critics alike. Instead of cutting clips on a timeline, you edit the transcript — delete words from the text, and the corresponding video is removed automatically. This paradigm shift makes video editing accessible to anyone who can type, collapsing hours of timeline work into minutes of text editing. The multi-track timeline supports collaborative editing in real time, so teams can work on the same project simultaneously, whether editing a podcast episode, a YouTube video, or a corporate training module.

## Transcript-Based Editing

At the core of Descript is its transcript-first approach. When you import or record media, Descript automatically transcribes the audio into editable text. Every word in the transcript is synchronized frame-accurately with the video and audio tracks. You can cut, copy, paste, or delete text just like in a word processor, and the corresponding media adjusts instantly. This makes removing mistakes, reordering sections, or trimming pauses as simple as editing a document. The filler word removal tool takes this further — with one click, Descript scans the transcript for "ums," "uhs," and long silences, and removes them all from the audio and video tracks simultaneously, saving hours of manual editing.

## AI Video and Audio Editing

Descript's AI suite extends far beyond transcription. **Studio Sound** analyzes your audio waveform and applies intelligent noise reduction, equalization, and compression to transform poor-quality microphone recordings into clean, studio-grade audio — even on recordings made in untreated rooms or noisy environments. **AI eye contact correction** repositions your gaze in the video so it appears you are looking directly at the camera, ideal for fixing recordings where the speaker was reading from a script off-screen. **Screen recording** with automatic transcription lets you capture tutorials, demos, and presentations with the transcript ready to edit as soon as you stop recording. Descript also supports export to all major formats and social platforms, directly publishing to YouTube, Vimeo, and social media channels without leaving the app.

## Voice Cloning with Overdub

One of Descript's most remarkable AI features is **Overdub**, a voice cloning system that lets you create a synthetic version of your own voice. After training on a short sample of recorded speech, Overdub can generate new vocal audio from typed text, matching your natural cadence, tone, and inflection. This is transformative for correcting flubbed lines without re-recording — simply type the correction in the transcript and Overdub inserts the fix seamlessly. You can also use Overdub to generate entirely new voice-over narration, create voice clones for team members (with permission), and even change individual words in a recording after the fact. The voice cloning is paired with AI-generated voices for cases where you need a professional announcer, character voice, or multilingual narration without hiring a voice actor.

## Pricing and Who It's For

Descript offers a tiered pricing model starting with a **Free** plan (1 hour of transcription per month, watermarked export), a **Hobbyist** plan at $24/month (10 hours of transcription, Studio Sound, no watermark, screen recording), and a **Business** plan at $40/month (unlimited transcription, team collaboration, brand kits, custom export presets). For podcasters, YouTubers, course creators, and anyone producing talking-head or interview-based content, Descript is the most efficient editing tool available. The workflow from recording to publishing is dramatically faster than traditional video editors like Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve, making it the go-to choice for creators who prioritize speed and accessibility over complex visual effects and animation capabilities.
