Frase is the go-to tool for SEO-focused content creators who want to write articles that actually rank. The workflow is simple: enter a target keyword, and Frase analyzes the top 20 search results to identify common questions, subtopics, recommended word count, and content structure. It then generates a content brief with everything you need to write a comprehensive, competitive article.

The AI writing assistant uses this research context to generate content that's optimized for the target keywords from the start. The content optimization score gives real-time feedback on how well your article covers the topics that Google expects. For content marketers and SEO writers, Frase eliminates the guesswork from search-optimized content creation.
