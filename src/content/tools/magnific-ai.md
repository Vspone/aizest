Magnific AI has quickly established itself as the gold standard for AI image upscaling. Unlike traditional upscalers that simply stretch pixels and apply a blur filter, Magnific uses generative AI to actually infer and add detail. A low-resolution photograph of a face doesn't just get bigger — the AI reconstructs skin texture, hair strands, fabric details, and lighting. The results can be startling, especially for portraits and detailed artwork.

The upscaler offers two main modes: Standard Upscale for clean, faithful enlargement, and Creative Enhancement which adds artistic detail and texture. The real-time preview slider lets you see exactly how much enhancement is being applied, giving you fine control over the result. Face restoration is a standout feature — old or low-res photos with faces that are barely recognizable can be transformed into sharp, usable portraits. For photographers, artists, and anyone working with legacy imagery, this alone can justify the subscription.

The major drawback is the pricing. At $39/month for the Pro plan, it's one of the more expensive AI tools on the market, and the free tier offers only 5 upscales at limited resolution. There's also no desktop app, so you're dependent on your browser. But if image quality is non-negotiable and you need maximum resolution with realistic detail, Magnific AI delivers results that nothing else quite matches.
