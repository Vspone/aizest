Notion AI is less about generating content from scratch and more about enhancing your existing workflow. It's embedded directly where you already work — in your notes, databases, and project pages.

The summarization feature is transformative for teams. Turn long meeting notes into concise action items, get the key points from a research document, or summarize a month of project updates in seconds.

The AI Q&A feature is powerful — ask 'What was our Q1 revenue target?' and it searches your entire workspace for the answer. It effectively eliminates the 'where did I write that?' problem that every knowledge worker faces.
