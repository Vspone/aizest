Sudowrite is the most capable AI writing tool built specifically for fiction authors. Unlike general-purpose assistants, Sudowrite understands story structure, character arcs, pacing, and narrative voice. Its suite of modes — Describe, Expand, Rewrite, and Outline — map directly to the tasks creative writers actually do: fleshing out a scene, adding sensory detail, fixing awkward prose, or planning a chapter.

The Story Engine feature is where Sudowrite really shines. You provide a premise and character notes, and it generates long-form narrative that maintains consistency across chapters. It's not a set-it-and-forget-it tool — the best results come from an iterative collaboration where the writer directs and the AI drafts. For authors on deadline or anyone wrestling with writer's block, Sudowrite can be a powerful creative partner.

Where Sudowrite falls short is in the polish department. It has no built-in grammar checker or proofreading tools, so you'll still need a separate editing pass. And while the Professional plan is reasonable for serious writers, the free tier is quite limited. For fiction authors looking to accelerate their drafting process without sacrificing creative control, however, Sudowrite is arguably the best tool available.
