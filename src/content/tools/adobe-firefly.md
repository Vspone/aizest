Adobe Firefly stands apart from other AI image generators because of how it was trained — entirely on licensed and public domain content. This means images generated with Firefly are safe for commercial use, a critical advantage for brands and agencies that can't risk copyright disputes.

Firefly is most powerful when used inside Adobe's ecosystem. Generative Fill in Photoshop lets you select any part of an image and replace it with AI-generated content that matches lighting, perspective, and style. The Text Effects feature turns lettering into anything from molten gold to woven fabric, opening up creative possibilities that were previously time-consuming.

For designers already in the Adobe ecosystem, Firefly is a no-brainer. The free tier gives you 25 credits to experiment, and the $4.99/mo Standard plan is affordable for occasional use. If you need unlimited generations and full Creative Cloud integration, the Pro plan is worth the investment.
