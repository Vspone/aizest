HubSpot has been adding AI capabilities across its entire platform, and the result is a powerful assistant for marketing and sales teams. The AI content generator can draft blog posts, social media updates, and email sequences based on your brand voice and goals. ChatSpot, the conversational CRM assistant, lets you ask questions like "show me deals closing this quarter worth over $10K" in plain English.

The AI lead scoring engine is one of the most valuable features. It analyzes historical deal data to predict which prospects are most likely to convert, so sales teams can focus their energy where it matters most. The SEO tools use AI to suggest topic clusters and identify content gaps based on what your competitors are ranking for.

For businesses already using HubSpot, the AI enhancements make an already powerful platform even better. The free tier includes enough to test the waters, and the Starter plan at $45/mo is reasonable for small teams. Enterprise users will want the Professional tier for the full AI toolkit.
