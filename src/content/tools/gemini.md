Google Gemini is Google's flagship AI model, designed to be a direct competitor to ChatGPT and Claude. What sets Gemini apart is its native multimodality — it can process text, images, audio, and video input simultaneously without needing separate models. The 1 million token context window lets you upload entire codebases, hours of meeting recordings, or complete research papers in a single conversation.

Gemini comes in two main variants: Gemini 2.5 Flash (fast, efficient, free) and Gemini 2.5 Pro (deep reasoning, Advanced subscription). The integration with Google Workspace makes it uniquely powerful — you can ask Gemini to summarize your Gmail inbox, draft a Doc, or analyze a Sheet without leaving your workflow.

For users who need long-context analysis, real-time web search, or a capable free AI assistant, Gemini is one of the strongest options available. The Advanced subscription at $19.99/mo undercuts ChatGPT Plus while offering a comparable feature set.
