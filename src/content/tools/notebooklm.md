NotebookLM by Google is one of the most innovative AI tools to emerge in recent years, reimagining what a note-taking app can be. Upload your documents — PDFs, Google Docs, websites, YouTube transcripts — and NotebookLM analyzes everything, then lets you ask questions that it answers using only your materials, with inline citations back to the source. No hallucinated facts, no speculation outside your documents.

The Audio Overview feature went viral for good reason: it turns your documents into an engaging AI-generated podcast conversation between two hosts who discuss, debate, and synthesize your content. For students studying for exams, researchers digesting papers, or professionals preparing for meetings, Audio Overview is a genuinely new way to consume and understand information. And it's completely free.
