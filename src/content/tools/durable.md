Durable is an AI website builder designed specifically for small businesses, freelancers, and service providers who need a professional online presence without the time, cost, or technical skill required for traditional web development. The platform's headline feature is its speed: describe your business and Durable's AI generates a complete website — including copy, images, layout, and SEO meta tags — in under 30 seconds.

Beyond the initial site build, Durable provides a suite of business tools that extend well beyond basic web hosting. The built-in CRM tracks leads and client communications, while the invoicing system lets you send quotes and collect payments directly through your site. AI-powered blog generation helps maintain fresh content for SEO, and the Google Business integration ensures your business appears in local search results. The platform also generates a matching logo and brand color palette.

Durable is best suited for solo entrepreneurs, contractors, and local businesses that need a clean, functional website quickly. The free tier is functional for testing, while the $12 per month Starter plan unlocks custom domains and unlimited AI blog posts. The main trade-off is customization depth — Durable's AI handles the heavy lifting, but users who want granular control over every design element may find the options limited. For simplicity and speed, however, Durable is one of the best options available.
