DeepSeek exploded onto the global AI scene in early 2025, shocking the industry by rivaling GPT-4 and Claude 3.5 at a fraction of the training cost. Created by the Chinese hedge fund High-Flyer, DeepSeek proved that top-tier AI could be built with far fewer resources than previously thought.

The R1 reasoning model's chain-of-thought transparency is particularly notable — it shows you its internal reasoning process step by step, building trust and making it easy to spot errors. For coding, math, and logical reasoning tasks, DeepSeek consistently scores at or near the top of benchmarks, often outperforming more expensive closed-source alternatives.

The free web tier is generous with no daily message limits, making it an excellent option for students, researchers, and developers. The open-weight release of V3 and R1 also made DeepSeek a favorite among the open-source AI community for self-hosted deployments.
