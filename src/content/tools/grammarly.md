Grammarly is one of the most widely used AI writing assistants, trusted by millions for its real-time grammar, spelling, and style correction capabilities. Available as a browser extension, desktop application, and mobile keyboard, Grammarly works across virtually every text field on the web — from emails and documents to social media posts and messaging apps. Its core strength lies in catching errors and suggesting improvements that go far beyond simple spell-check.

Beyond basic corrections, Grammarly Premium offers advanced features including tone detection, clarity improvements, conciseness suggestions, and genre-specific writing goals. Whether you're drafting a formal business proposal, a casual email, or an academic paper, Grammarly adjusts its recommendations to match the intended style and audience. The plagiarism checker is an additional valuable tool for students and professionals verifying content originality.

Grammarly's greatest value is as a polish and refinement layer for existing writing rather than a content generator. It shines in professional settings where clear, error-free communication is essential. The free tier handles basic grammar and spelling, making it accessible to everyone, while the premium tiers unlock deeper stylistic analysis that helps writers develop more confident and effective communication habits.
