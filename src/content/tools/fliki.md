Fliki is one of the most popular text-to-video platforms, and for good reason. It takes written content — blog posts, articles, scripts, or plain text — and turns it into a narrated video complete with AI voiceover, background music, and matching stock footage. The workflow is simple: paste your text, choose an AI voice, and let the platform automatically match scenes to your content. It's particularly useful for content creators who want to repurpose written material into video format for YouTube, social media, or educational content.

Fliki's main strength is its voice library, offering over 2000 AI voices across 80 languages. The premium voices are remarkably natural, though the standard voices still have that slight AI cadence that becomes noticeable in longer videos. The stock media library is extensive, and the auto-matching algorithm does a decent job at finding relevant footage. You can also upload your own media for more control. The custom branding feature lets you add logos and brand colors for consistency across videos.

The biggest downside is the restrictive pricing. The free tier allows only 5 minutes of video per month, which is barely enough to test the platform. The Standard plan at $28/month is reasonable for casual use, but the Premium plan jumps to $88/month for unlimited access. While Fliki excels at turning text into simple explainer videos and social content, it's not built for complex narratives, animations, or anything requiring precise creative control.
