Fireflies AI is a meeting intelligence platform that goes beyond simple transcription. It connects to Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, and Webex, as well as voice calls and even Slack audio clips. Once connected, Fireflies automatically joins your meetings, records them, and creates searchable transcripts that you can access from anywhere.

What sets Fireflies apart is its conversation intelligence layer. It tracks talk-to-listen ratios, sentiment trends, and specific keywords across all your meetings. Sales teams can set up topic tracking for competitor mentions or pricing discussions and get alerts when those topics come up. The "Ask Fred" feature lets you query your entire meeting history in natural language — "what did the client say about pricing in last week's call?"

For sales and customer success teams, Fireflies is a powerful tool for capturing insights that would otherwise fall through the cracks. The Pro plan at $10/mo is excellent value for unlimited recordings, and the Business plan adds conversation analytics that larger teams will find invaluable.
