Midjourney produces some of the most visually stunning AI-generated images available. Its artistic quality consistently surpasses competitors, especially for creative and conceptual work.

The tool operates entirely through Discord, which has built a strong community atmosphere but can be confusing for new users. The prompt engineering requires practice — learning parameters like aspect ratio, stylization, and chaos is key.

V6 and newer models have significantly improved prompt adherence and photorealism. The style reference feature lets you maintain visual consistency across a series of images, making it practical for branding projects.
