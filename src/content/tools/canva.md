Canva has evolved from a simple design tool into a comprehensive AI-powered visual communication platform. The Magic Design feature generates complete designs from a text description — type 'modern Instagram post for a coffee shop' and get multiple polished options.

The AI features are woven throughout the platform. Magic Eraser removes unwanted objects from photos, background removal works in one click, and the AI image generator creates original visuals matching your style.

For non-designers, Canva is unbeatable. The learning curve is minimal, and the results look professional. Even professional designers use Canva for quick mockups and social media content.
