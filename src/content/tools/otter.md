Otter.ai has become an essential tool for anyone who spends their days in meetings. It joins your Zoom, Google Meet, or Teams calls as a participant, records the entire conversation, and delivers a searchable transcript with speaker labels in real time. After the meeting, Otter generates an AI summary that captures key decisions, action items, and follow-ups.

The slide capture feature is particularly clever — when someone shares their screen during a presentation, Otter automatically captures slides and inserts them into the transcript at the right moment. This means you never have to scramble to screenshot a slide while also trying to listen. The searchable archive lets you find any mention of a topic or person across hundreds of meetings instantly.

For teams that want to stay aligned and never lose track of what was decided, Otter is worth every penny. The free tier is great for trying it out, but the Pro plan at $16.99/mo is where the tool becomes truly useful for daily meeting workflows.
