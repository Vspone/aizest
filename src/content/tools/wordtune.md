Wordtune is a focused AI writing tool that excels at one thing: helping you find better ways to say what you already mean. Unlike broad AI assistants that generate content from scratch, Wordtune takes your existing sentences and offers alternative phrasings in different tones and lengths. This makes it ideal for polishing emails, sharpening business communications, or refining academic writing.

The browser extension is where Wordtune feels most natural. It integrates with Gmail, LinkedIn, Google Docs, and most web-based text editors. You highlight a sentence, click a button, and Wordtune presents several rewritten versions — shorter, longer, more formal, or more casual. The AI reading companion is a nice bonus, letting you paste article links or PDFs for AI-generated summaries. This feature alone makes it useful for researchers and professionals who process a lot of reading material.

Wordtune isn't built for fiction writers or anyone needing help with structure or long-form content. It's a surface-level refinement tool, and it's very good at that job. The free tier is barely usable at 10 rewrites per day, but the Premium plan is reasonably priced for professionals who write a lot and want every sentence to land better.
